---
title: "Variational Inference for Marginal Longitudinal Semiparametric Regression"
collection: publications
venue: 'Stat'
---

We derive a variational inference procedure for approximate Bayesian inference in marginal longitudinal semiparametric regression. Fitting and inference is much faster than existing Markov chain Monte Carlo approaches. Numerical studies indicate that the new methodology is very accurate for the class of models
under consideration.
